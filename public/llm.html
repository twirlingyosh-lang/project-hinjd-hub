Hinjd Global - Industrial Conveyor Solutions

Company Overview

Hinjd Global is an industrial technology company specializing in conveyor belt diagnostics and protection systems. The company serves mining, aggregate, cement, power generation, and bulk material handling industries worldwide.

Products and Services

BeltSaver™ Tail Pulley Protection System

BeltSaver is a patented conveyor belt protection system (U.S. Patent No. 12,195,281 B1) designed to prevent belt misalignment at the tail pulley.

Key Features:

  • Clamp-on retrofit design - no welding required
  • Two semi-circular discs bolt around existing shafts
  • Extends 4-6 inches above belt for guidance
  • Installation time: less than 4 hours
  • Extends belt life up to 3x
  • 5-year limited warranty
  • MSHA Approved, CE Marked

Technical Specifications:

  • Pulley Diameter Range: 12" - 48" (305mm - 1220mm)
  • Belt Width Compatibility: 18" - 72" (457mm - 1829mm)
  • Max Belt Speed: 1,200 FPM (6.1 m/s)
  • Operating Temperature: -40°F to 180°F (-40°C to 82°C)
  • Material: High-durometer polyurethane / Steel core

AggregateOpps - Conveyor Diagnostics Platform

A web-based diagnostic tool for analyzing conveyor belt tracking issues. Helps identify causes of belt mistracking and recommends solutions including BeltSaver implementation.
